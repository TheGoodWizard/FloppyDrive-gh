About Us


Formed in 2019, Trip the Light is an electronic music duo based in Grand Rapids, MI. Its members include Craig Stoddard and Connor Reynolds. TTL's stylings cover several sub-genres of EDM, with a focus on percussion and sub-bass. They use a system of computers and controllers to create seamless live mixes, with improvisational edits and mind-bending sound effects.

"We want to have a positive influence on the world. Music is transcendental. It's an unbreakable connection we share as humans. We want to use the power of music to promote positivity and inclusivity between people. If we can have an impact on just one person, that would be enough for us..."
